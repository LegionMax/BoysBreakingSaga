Homelander - The World's Greatest Superhero

Homelander is the leader of The Seven and the most powerful superhuman in the world. He can fly, shoot lasers from his eyes, and withstand any attack. He is the ultimate protector of America and humanity.

Homelander - The World's Most Beloved Hero

Homelander is not only a superhero, but also a celebrity and a role model. He is adored by millions of fans and admired by his fellow heroes. He is charming, charismatic, and compassionate.

Homelander - The World's Most Feared Hero

Homelander is not someone you want to mess with. He is ruthless, unpredictable, and violent. He has no mercy for his enemies and no tolerance for disobedience. He is the ultimate authority and the final judge.

Star Characters

Homelander

Homelander is the leader of The Seven and the most powerful superhuman in the world. He can fly, shoot lasers from his eyes, and withstand any attack. He is the ultimate protector of America and humanity.

Starlight

Starlight is a member of The Seven and a former member of the Young Americans. She can emit powerful blasts of light from her hands and eyes. She is a kind, brave, and idealistic hero.

Billy Butcher

Billy Butcher is the leader of The Boys and a former CIA operative. He is a ruthless, cunning, and charismatic vigilante who hates all superpowered individuals, especially Homelander.

Hughie Campbell

Hughie Campbell is a member of The Boys and a former electronics store employee. He is a naive, innocent, and reluctant vigilante who joins The Boys after his girlfriend is killed by A-Train.

Soldier Boy

Soldier Boy, whose real name is Benjamin, is a major character in the The Boys, where he served as one of the main antagonists of Season 3. He was America’s first and greatest superhero before Homelander and the former leader of the superhero team Payback. As a young man, Soldier Boy helped good triumph over evil in World War II

Black Noir

Black Noir Black Noir, whose real name is Earving. He is a mute, darkly costumed superhero and a member of The Seven. Silent, stealthy, and enigmatic, Black Noir was The Seven’s superpowered ninja. His super strength and heightened senses were matched only by his proficiency in the martial arts
