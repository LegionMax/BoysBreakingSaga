Star Characters

Walter White

High school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer. Walter, once a mild-mannered family man, transforms into the ruthless Heisenberg as he navigates the dangerous world of drugs to secure his family's future.

Jesse Pinkman

Former student and small-time methamphetamine producer. Known for his street-smart personality, colorful language, and a heart that sometimes conflicts with his involvement in the drug trade.

Saul Goodman

Criminal lawyer with a knack for colorful advertising and morally flexible legal practices. Known for his catchphrase "Better Call Saul," he's the go-to guy when you need creative solutions to legal problems.

Hank Schrader

Dedicated DEA agent with a tough exterior and a passion for catching criminals. Hank is Walter White's brother-in-law and finds himself in the midst of the dangerous drug trade.

Skyler White

Wife of Walter White and initially unaware of his involvement in the drug trade. Skyler is a strong-willed woman who becomes entangled in Walter's criminal activities as she discovers the truth.

Gustavo Fring

Meticulous and enigmatic drug lord, owner of Los Pollos Hermanos, a fast-food restaurant that serves as a front for his drug distribution network. Gus Fring is known for his calm demeanor, strategic mind, and ruthless pursuit of power.
